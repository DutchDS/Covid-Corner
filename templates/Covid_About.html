About Us

During the late span of the Data Analytics Boot Camp, our team saw a need to create some comfort during times of turmoil. We saw the ever-increasing interest of the highly talked about COVID-19 virus. Everywhere you go, you hear something about the virus. Either how the virus spreads, how the virus attacks the body, or how many the virus has killed. If someone has symptoms, but don't know if they should call a doctor. We want to ease any worries the person may have by creating a model that would suggest if you need to contact any medical services. Our goal is to help people, while still being able to stay at home.
